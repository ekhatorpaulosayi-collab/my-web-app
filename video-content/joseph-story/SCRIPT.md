## PACING NOTES

- Fast cuts in "chaos" section (0:15-0:30) - 3 second max per shot
- Slow down for app demo (0:45-1:15) - Let viewers see the interface
- Emotional music swell at 1:15 when showing results
- End with uplifting music for CTA

## TONE & STYLE

**NOT:** Corporate, salesy, over-produced
**YES:** Authentic, relatable, inspiring

**Visual Style:** Raw, slightly handheld feel for Joseph scenes. Clean screen recordings for app demo.

**Color Grade:** Warm tones. Make Joseph's store feel familiar and inviting.

## KEY MESSAGES

1. **Relatable Problem** - Every small shop owner faces this
2. **Simple Solution** - Not intimidating tech, just helpful
3. **Real Results** - Specific numbers, believable transformation
4. **Low Barrier** - Free to start, works on any phone
